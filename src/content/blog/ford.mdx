Henry Ford's innovations transformed American industry:

- Moving assembly line (1913)
- $5 workday (1914)
- Model T production (15 million sold)
- Vertical integration (River Rouge Plant)

Ford's methods reduced Model T production time from 12 hours to 93 minutes while cutting the price from $850 to $260.
