Major Cold War flashpoints included:

- Truman Doctrine (1947)
- Berlin Airlift (1948-49)
- Korean War (1950-53)
- Cuban Missile Crisis (1962)
- Vietnam War (1955-75)
- Space Race (1957-69)

The Cold War shaped American foreign policy for decades and spurred technological innovation.
