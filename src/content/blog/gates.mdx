Bill Gates co-founded Microsoft in 1975, leading to:

- MS-DOS (1981)
- Windows (1985)
- Office Suite (1989)
- Internet Explorer (1995)

Microsoft's success made Gates the world's richest person and transformed how businesses operate globally.
