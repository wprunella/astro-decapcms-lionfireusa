Franklin Roosevelt's New Deal (1933-1939) introduced unprecedented federal programs:

- Social Security Act
- Works Progress Administration
- Tennessee Valley Authority
- Federal Deposit Insurance Corporation
- Securities and Exchange Commission

These reforms established America's modern social safety net and regulatory framework.
